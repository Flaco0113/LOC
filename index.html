How LOC works

Fantasy sports for entire teams

LOC blends draft strategy, postseason prediction, league rivalries, and commissioner controls into one cross-sport competition.

1

Create or join a league

Commissioners set the season, sports, participant count, draft date, and invitations.

2

Draft one team per sport

Managers use a snake draft to select eligible teams across NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, NCAA, UEFA, NASCAR, and golf.

3

Track postseason results

Standings update as drafted teams advance, lose, win titles, or earn runner-up bonuses.

4

Crown the LOC champion

Final rankings total every drafted team across every selected sport.

Platform benefits

Built like a real fantasy hub

Users get personal dashboards, league cards, invitations, draft results, team pages, live standings, scoring tabs, and activity feeds. Commissioners get the admin controls they need to keep a league fair and organized.

Role-based accessLeague content stays behind account verification.
Real-time readyDraft room and standings surfaces are structured for live updates.
Audit-friendlyDraft changes, score overrides, and settings updates are logged.
Future-proof sportsThe universal scoring engine supports new leagues without new rules.

Scoring system

One scoring language for every sport

Placement points run from 9 points for first place to 1 point for ninth. Champions earn +3, runners-up earn +1.

FAQ

Common questions

Do users draft players?

No. LOC managers draft entire teams, then score based on postseason success.

Can commissioners invite users?

Yes. Private leagues support email invitations, pending invite tracking, and user management.

How are ties handled?

Tiebreakers use furthest advancement, elimination margin, elimination-round offense, and higher playoff seed.

Is public league support available?

Public leagues are marked as a future feature in this prototype.
